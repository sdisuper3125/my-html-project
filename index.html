Digital Welfare System

This interface powers our Community Hubs. It serves as the critical "Trust Layer" that verifies net collection and facilitates the exchange of waste for welfare.

1. Data Entry & Verification

Hub managers weigh incoming nets. IoT-connected scales log the weight (kg) directly to our blockchain ledger, preventing data tampering.

2. Instant Value Transfer

Algorithm converts weight to Net-Weave Credits (15 credits/kg). Fishers can instantly redeem these for pre-vetted goods (food, insurance, energy).
